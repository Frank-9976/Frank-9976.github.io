okay but what is this concept

Let $A$ be an abelian group. Let $\varphi \colon A^\omega \rightarrow A$ be a linear map from the direct product of countably many copies of $A$ to $A$ itself. We can regard $\varphi$ as an operation of countable arity. For the sake of having names, say that $(A, \varphi)$ is an "$\omega$-module". This is a dumb name, but it will have to do.
